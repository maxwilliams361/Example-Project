We Must Act Now!

Below are some useful tips of what YOU can do to combat climate change and protect the enviornment

Use less energy

The following are things you can do every day to consume less energy:

  1. Turn off lights
  2. Take shorter showers
  3. Unplug unused electronics

Use a reusable bag when shopping for groceries

Reduce the amount of plastic that is produced and used by bringing your own reusable bag when you go shopping

Write to your representatives

Write to your representatives to let them know that combating climate change is important to you, and explaining what issues they should be taking action on. Here is a link to find who your representative is
